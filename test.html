üóì Step 3 ‚Äì Planner & Daily Jobs

Once logged in, follow these steps exactly to start a job:

  1. Tap View Jobs.
  2. Select the Job you are working on.
  3. You will see the Lot Address and job details.
  4. Tap Check In and open the Location Map.
  5. Tap the bottom-left button on the map.
  6. Tap Validate.
  7. Tap the bottom-right checkmark to confirm check-in.

Before starting work:

  1. Go back into the Job.
  2. Tap the Attachments button (bottom right).
  3. Tap the arrow next to Before Photos.
  4. Upload all required before photos before starting the job.

üì∑ Step 4 ‚Äì Check Out & After Photos

After finishing all work:

  1. Tap Check Out and open the Location Map.
  2. Follow the same process as check-in:
    • Tap the bottom-left button on the map.
    • Tap Validate.
    • Tap the bottom-right checkmark to confirm check-out.

After checking out:

  1. Go back into the Job.
  2. Tap the Attachments button (bottom right).
  3. Tap the arrow next to After Photos.
  4. Upload all required after photos.
  5. Once photos are uploaded, tap the checkmark to finalize the job.
