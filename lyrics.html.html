Navidrome Album LRC Finder v4

Search an album, find synced .lrc files, and export them using either a normal pattern or your exact local audio filenames. For Navidrome, exact audio filename matching is usually the difference between “lyrics found” and “no lyrics available.”
Uses Apple/iTunes Search for album tracklists and LRCLIB for synced lyrics. No account needed.

Filename Matching

This reads filenames only. The app will export LRC files like 04 - Sabrina Carpenter - Sugar Talking.lrc when your audio file is 04 - Sabrina Carpenter - Sugar Talking.opus. You can also build the whole tracklist from your selected files.
